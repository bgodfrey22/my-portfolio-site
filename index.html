A little about me.

I am a freelance developer currently based out of Miami, Florida. My journey into tech began with a passion for helping others achieve their goals as a teacher and as a coach. During my career in education, I realized I wanted to learn how to design and code, and I believe it is never too late to start something new.

When I built my first website, the excitement I felt assured me that I was headed in the right direction. Now I want to share that excitement with others that have their own goals and want to help them bring their own ideas to life online. My organization, attention to detail, and diligence allow me to do that successfully.
